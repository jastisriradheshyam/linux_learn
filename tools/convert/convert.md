# Convert

A ImageMagick tool

## Resize the image

- `convert -resize 320x240 FROM_IMAGE_PATH.(png/svg) FINAL_IMAGE_PATH.(png/svg)`
- `convert -resize 50% FROM_IMAGE_PATH.(png/svg) FINAL_IMAGE_PATH.(png/svg)`
